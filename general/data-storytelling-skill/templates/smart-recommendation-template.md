# SMART Recommendation Template

**Purpose:** Transform vague ideas into actionable recommendations with quantified impact.

**SMART = Specific, Measurable, Achievable, Relevant, Time-bound**

---

## Standard Format

### Recommendation Title
[Action Verb] + [What] + [Expected Outcome]

**Examples:**
- ✅ "Launch 10-15 new SKU per quarter to recover Rp 500M revenue"
- ✅ "Open 5 tourism stores by Q4 to capture Rp 7.5B annual revenue"
- ❌ "Improve product portfolio" (vague)
- ❌ "Expand stores" (no specifics)

---

### Components (Required)

**1. WHAT (Specific)**
- [ ] Exact action to take
- [ ] Scope clearly defined
- [ ] No ambiguity

**2. WHY (Relevant)**
- [ ] Ties to root cause
- [ ] Addresses the problem/opportunity
- [ ] Strategic rationale

**3. HOW (Achievable)**
- [ ] Implementation approach
- [ ] Key steps/phases
- [ ] Resource requirements

**4. IMPACT (Measurable)**
- [ ] Quantified outcome
- [ ] Revenue/profit/growth target
- [ ] Success metrics

**5. WHEN (Time-bound)**
- [ ] Start date
- [ ] Key milestones
- [ ] Completion deadline

**6. INVESTMENT**
- [ ] Capex/opex required
- [ ] Budget breakdown
- [ ] Source of funds

**7. ROI**
- [ ] Return on investment
- [ ] Payback period
- [ ] Break-even timeline

**8. RISK MITIGATION**
- [ ] Key risks identified
- [ ] Mitigation strategies
- [ ] Contingency plans

**9. OWNER**
- [ ] Who executes
- [ ] Accountable party
- [ ] Supporting teams

---


## Example: Product Refresh

### Launch New SKU Pipeline to Recover Declining Revenue

**What:**
Launch 10-15 new SKU per quarter starting Q2 2025
- Scope: Men's & Baby categories (proven high performers)
- Deliverables: Fresh designs, new colors, trend-based styles
- Pilot: 3 SKU in April before full rollout

**Why:**
Addresses product lifecycle maturity and market saturation
- Root cause: Top 20 SKU are 2-3 years old, customer fatigue
- Proof point: Merci SKU launched Q4 2024 grew +30.9% YoY
- Market gap: Competitors launching new designs quarterly, we're falling behind

**How:**
1. Design phase (March 2025)
   - R&D team designs 15 SKU concepts
   - User testing with focus groups
   - Finalize 12-15 designs for production
   
2. Pilot launch (April 2025)
   - Produce 3 SKU (small batch)
   - Launch in top 5 stores
   - Measure customer response (2 weeks)
   
3. Full rollout (May-June 2025)
   - Production ramp for remaining 12 SKU
   - National distribution
   - Marketing campaign push

**Impact:**
- Primary: Recover Rp 500M revenue by Q3 2025
- Secondary: Refresh brand perception, attract younger customers
- Success metric: 30% of new SKU contribute >Rp 30M each within 6 months

**Timeline:**
- Start: March 1, 2025
- Milestones:
  - March 31: Designs finalized
  - April 15: Pilot launch
  - May 1: Full production begins
  - June 30: All 15 SKU in market
- Complete: Q3 2025 (measure success)

**Investment:**
- Total: Rp 150M
- Breakdown:
  - Design & development: Rp 30M
  - Initial production: Rp 90M
  - Marketing campaign: Rp 30M

**ROI:**
- Expected return: Rp 500M revenue by Q3
- Payback period: 4-5 months
- ROI multiple: 3.3x within 6 months

**Risk Mitigation:**
- Risk #1: New SKU flop (customer rejection)
  → Mitigation: Pilot 3 SKU first, measure response before full rollout
  
- Risk #2: Cannibalization (new SKU eat old SKU sales)
  → Mitigation: Phase out declining SKU deliberately, monitor overlap
  
- Risk #3: Production delays
  → Mitigation: Start design early (March), buffer timeline, backup suppliers

**Owner:** Product Development Team (Mbak Dewi)  
**Supporting:** R&D, Marketing, Ops

## Quick Checklist

Before presenting a recommendation:

- [ ] Can explain in one sentence (elevator pitch)
- [ ] Quantified impact (revenue/profit/growth)
- [ ] Timeline with specific dates
- [ ] Investment amount clear
- [ ] ROI calculated
- [ ] Implementation approach outlined (not just "do it")
- [ ] Risks identified + mitigation strategies
- [ ] Owner assigned
- [ ] Ties back to root cause/strategic problem
- [ ] Success metrics defined

## Common Weak Recommendations → Fixed

### Example 1: Product Development

**❌ Weak:**
"We should launch new products to improve sales."

**✅ Strong:**
"Launch 10-15 new SKU per quarter in Men's & Baby categories starting Q2 2025. Investment: Rp 150M. Expected recovery: Rp 500M by Q3. ROI: 3.3x. Pilot 3 SKU first to mitigate risk."

---

### Example 2: Store Expansion

**❌ Weak:**
"Expand to tourism areas because they perform well."

**✅ Strong:**
"Open 5 stores in proven tourism hotspots (Canggu, Seminyak, Labuan Bajo x2, Raja Ampat) by Q3-Q4 2025. Bali/Lombok model proven at 2-3x urban productivity. Investment: Rp 1B (Rp 200M per store). Expected revenue: Rp 7.5B/year. Payback: 14-16 months per store. Start with Canggu/Seminyak (lowest risk), then expand."

---

### Example 3: Digital Marketing

**❌ Weak:**
"Invest more in online marketing."

**✅ Strong:**
"Scale online channel 50% YoY (Rp 22.9B → Rp 34B) via Rp 200M/year digital marketing investment. Channels: SEO/SEM (Rp 50M/quarter), marketplace optimization, exclusive online SKU, influencer partnerships (10-15 micro-influencers/month). ROI: 55x (incremental Rp 11B revenue vs Rp 200M investment). Track monthly GMV, conversion rates, CAC."

## Why This Format Works

**For Decision-Makers:**
- Quickly assess feasibility (WHAT + HOW)
- Understand trade-offs (INVESTMENT vs IMPACT)
- Evaluate risk (MITIGATION strategies)
- Assign accountability (OWNER)

**For Implementation:**
- Clear action steps (HOW)
- Timeline with milestones (WHEN)
- Resource requirements (INVESTMENT)
- Success criteria (METRICS)

**For You:**
- Forces clarity (can't hide behind vague language)
- Ensures completeness (all key questions answered)
- Builds credibility (shows you've thought it through)
