# Executive Summary Template

**Purpose:** One-slide summary that answers "So what?" before diving into details.

---

## Structure

### Header
**Headline:** [Provocative conclusion, not topic]  
**Subheadline:** [Context/timeframe]

---

### The Challenge (Red box)
**Icon:** 🚨  
**Metric:** [Key negative metric]  
**Description:** [What's wrong/at risk + quantified impact]

**Example:**
```
🚨 THE CHALLENGE
-83% YoY Decline
19 dari 20 top SKU mengalami penurunan drastis. 
Tanpa intervensi, kita kehilangan Rp 2.3B revenue by Q4 2025.
```

---

### The Opportunity (Green box)
**Icon:** ✅  
**Metric:** [Key positive metric/untapped potential]  
**Description:** [What's working/available + proof point]

**Example:**
```
✅ THE OPPORTUNITY
Rp 33B Untapped
Bali/Lombok avg Rp 1.5-2.7B per store (2x Jakarta). 
Online channel tumbuh Rp 11.4B per channel. Tourism model proven.
```

---

### The Bottom Line (Blue box)
**Icon:** 📊  
**3 Key Metrics:**
- Current state metric
- Target/goal metric
- Deadline/urgency metric

**Example:**
```
📊 The Bottom Line
Rp 2.9B - Top 20 SKU Revenue (declining)
Rp 50.4B - Total YTD Revenue (Bali + Online dominant)
Q2 2025 - Deadline for product refresh
```

---

### Our Recommendation (Teal/Green gradient box)
**Icon:** 🎯  
**Format:** 3-column grid (3 plays/priorities)

**Each play includes:**
- **Title:** Brief action name
- **Description:** 1 sentence what + expected outcome

**Example:**
```
🎯 Our Recommendation: 3-Play Strategy

Play #1: Product Refresh
Launch 10-15 new SKU per quarter → Recover Rp 500M by Q3

Play #2: Geographic Expansion
Add 5 stores in tourism areas → Rp 7.5B additional revenue/year

Play #3: Digital Acceleration
Scale online 50% YoY → Rp 34B by end of 2025
```

---

## Checklist

Before finalizing executive summary:

- [ ] Headline is a conclusion (not "Executive Summary")
- [ ] Challenge quantified (how much at risk?)
- [ ] Opportunity quantified (how much upside?)
- [ ] Recommendation specific (not generic "improve X")
- [ ] Timeline/deadline clear
- [ ] All metrics tied to business outcomes (revenue/profit/growth)
- [ ] Fits on one slide (no scrolling)
- [ ] Can standalone (executive might only read this slide)

---

## Why This Works

**Busy executives:**
- Read top slide first (may not continue)
- Need answer upfront (no time for buildup)
- Care about: What's wrong? What's the opportunity? What should we do?
- Want numbers (quantified impact)

**This template:**
- Answers all questions in 30 seconds
- Creates urgency (challenge + deadline)
- Shows upside (opportunity + ROI)
- Provides clear path (3 plays)
